## About This Blog

This is a food blog celebrating the art of cooking, with a focus on Persian cuisine and international flavors.

Each recipe comes with a story—the inspiration, the technique, and the joy of sharing food.

---

**Sirosabzi** (سیر سبزی) is a traditional Persian herb and garlic blend, a simple yet essential element of Persian cooking. Like the blend itself, this blog combines various flavors, techniques, and stories into something meaningful.

*Food is memory. Food is love. Food is home.*
